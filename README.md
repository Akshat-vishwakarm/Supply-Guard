#**SupplyGuard: AI-Powered Supply Chain Risk Assessment**
*SupplyGuard* is an advanced AI platform designed to provide end-to-end visibility and risk mitigation for global shipments. By aggregating real-time data and applying predictive modeling, it empowers logistics stakeholders to navigate volatile global trade routes with confidence.



**Key Features**
**End-to-End Risk Assessment:** Monitors shipments across multiple global routes and ports to identify vulnerabilities before they impact the bottom line.

**Multi-Factor Data Aggregation:** Integrates diverse data streams including:

**Economic:** Tax & tariff changes, currency fluctuations.

**Environmental:** Real-time weather disruptions.

**Logistics:** Port availability and congestion.

**Geopolitical:** Regional unrest and policy shifts.

**Intelligent Risk Scoring:** A proprietary model that evaluates and compares alternative routes and ports for any given destination.

**Financial Impact Estimation:** Quantifies potential financial loss by calculating the intersection of risk probability and disruption impact.

**Automated Analytical Reporting:** Generates data-driven reports to streamline executive decision-making.

**Resilience Visualization:** Interactive dashboards that visualize risk factors, helping users choose the most cost-effective and resilient paths.
